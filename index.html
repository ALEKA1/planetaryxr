PlanetaryAR

There are two components of this app, augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality via WebVR.

AR App

Using AR.js, PlanetaryAR can project planets as augmented reality models. Simply visit the app on a browser which supports WebGL and WebRTC and point your device's camera at any of the PlanetaryAR hiro codes (coming soon).

WebVR App

Using A-Frame, PlanetaryAR builds upon the simple 3D models in the AR app by offering a more complete map interface.

Taking locational data from NASA, USGS and other organisations working in planetary mapping, PlanetaryAR offers a virtual-reality planetary mapping service which also works seamlessly on non-VR browsers.
