PlanetaryAR

PlanetaryAR is an AR / WebVR web app featuring 3D maps of planetary bodies. The app uses A-Frame, ARToolKit and the AR.js library.

All locational data is currently taken from NASA and USGS.

Although primarily focused on augmented reality, there are two components of PlanetaryAR: augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR).

AR

PlanetaryAR projects planets as augmented reality models onto specially-designed markers. Simply visit the app on a browser which supports WebGL and WebRTC and point your device's camera at any of the markers (coming soon).

VR

The 3D planetary models used in PlanetaryAR can also be accessed in a WebVR interface, allowing the models to be viewed either in-browser or with a virtual reality device.

Eventually, PlanetaryAR will build upon these 3D models by offering a more complete map interface.
